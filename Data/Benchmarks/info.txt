## Online Bin Packing with Predictions  

Benchmarks from the BPP Bin Packing library that are used in the experiments. 
http://or.dei.unibo.it/library/bpplib

There is also an additional "Weibull" benchmark which contains sequences with Weibull distribution with different shape parameters (as described in Section 6.2. of the paper).
